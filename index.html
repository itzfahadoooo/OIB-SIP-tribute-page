Guillermo Rauch

The Visionary Behind Next.js

Guillermo Rauch is an innovative software engineer and entrepreneur, best known for creating Next.js, the powerful React framework. He is also the founder of Vercel, a company focused on providing the best developer experience for building modern web applications.

His work has revolutionized the way developers build web applications, enabling better performance, scalability, and server-side rendering with ease. Guillermo is a strong advocate for open-source projects and has contributed significantly to the developer community.

Early Life & Career

Guillermo started his journey as a Linux advocate and soon developed a passion for JavaScript and web development. At 18 years old, he joined the MooTools core team and later moved to San Francisco, where he began his professional career.

Notable Contributions

  • Next.js: The most popular React framework, powering apps like Twitch, Netflix, and TikTok.
  • Vercel: A cloud platform that simplifies deployment and scaling of modern applications.
  • Socket.IO: A real-time communication library used by Notion and Coinbase.
  • Mongoose: The most widely used MongoDB ODM for JavaScript.
  • Hyper: A cross-platform terminal emulator built with web technologies.
